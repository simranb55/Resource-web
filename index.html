Motion Preset Downloads

Presets are pre-made effects and animations that users can instantly apply to layers in their After Effects project,
it can efficiently save time and effort by automating motion designs!

Works for all After Effects Versions
Works for Windows & iOS

A quick tutorial on how to import the chosen presets to After Effects!

For iOS and Windows.

Step 1: Locate the downloaded preset in your download folder, copy the preset or open a new finder tab (this will be useful for later).

Step 2: Head over to the Documents tab and locate Adobe > the year of After effects you are using (Ex. After Effects 2023, After Effects 2024...) > User Persets.

Step 3: Under User Presets, create a new folder give it a name (Ex. Presets).

Step 4: Open the new folder you created and either drag and drop or paste (if you had copied) the preset file to this folder.

Step 5: Open After Effects (if the application is already open, close and reopen) and locate the Effects and Presets Pannel. Type in the name of the preset and it will appear! More information will be on the description of the preset about how it can be used!
